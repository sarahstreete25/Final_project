Artist Statement.

When I put my pencil on a piece of paper, I always end up drawing a girl. A 'wierd' one....who resembles Elizabeth Layton drawings but younger. I often ask myself why? What is it that drives me to create these unique beings? I don't know who they are or where they come from yet. But they are powerful and non-conforming tosociety norms. They're different and I like it. They dont have an identity and that's fine. They live out loud, in color, no pronouns, and no rules. Whether drawing, sewing, painting, sculpting, or mixed media, my mission is to add beauty in everything I touch. I am art.
